Our story

Stream Clean Limited was formed as a family business in 2003 and since then provides a bespoke, tailor made cleaning service to both domestic and commercial clients.

The founders of the business are proactive to this day and remain focused on providing clients with personalised, reputable and top quality service.

Our services include Pre and After Tenancy cleaning, Carpet, Curtain and Upholstery cleaning, hard floor polishing, interior and exterior Window cleaning, Post builders cleaning. Our attention to detail and our friendly, experienced and fully insured staff have managed to create a thriving business with an impeccable reputation. We have the privilege to work with some of the leading Estate Agencies in London and with some large building companies. We are experienced in cleaning all type of commercial premises including schools, doctor surgeries, factories, building sites, etc. We can be hired on a one-off basis or on a contract.
